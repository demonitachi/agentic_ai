# Top Italian Restaurants in Bangalore

## Little Italy
- **Location:** 100 Feet Road, HAL 2nd Stage, Indiranagar, Bangalore  
- **Description:** Known for its diverse menu and authentic dishes, Little Italy has a strong reputation for quality Italian cuisine.

## Chianti
- **Location:** Multiple branches in Bangalore  
- **Description:** Celebrated for its Neapolitan pizzas and handcrafted pastas, Chianti is a must-visit for Italian food lovers.
