# Banoffee Pie Recipe

## Description
An easy family favorite with buttery pastry and sweet dulce de leche. Recommended to serve with a generous dollop of cream.

## Serves
8 - 10

## Preparation Time
- Prep: 30 mins  
- Cook: 30-35 mins

## Ingredients
### For the Pastry
- 100g chilled butter (plus extra for greasing)  
- 200g plain flour  
- 1 medium egg (separated)  
- 1 tbsp golden caster sugar

### For the Filling
- 4 sliced bananas  
- 394g caramel or dulce de leche  
- 300ml double cream  
- Dark chocolate (optional, for garnish)

## Method
1. **Make the Pastry:**  
   Put the butter and flour in a food processor and pulse until it resembles fresh breadcrumbs. Add the yolk of the egg and the sugar, then pulse again. Gradually add very cold water until the dough starts to come together.  
2. **Chill the Dough:**  
   Form the dough into a ball, wrap in cling film, and chill in the fridge for 30 minutes.  
3. **Roll Out the Pastry:**  
   Preheat your oven to 190C/170C fan/gas 4. Grease a 23cm tart tin and roll out the chilled pastry. Line the tin with it, leaving some pastry hanging over the edge.  
4. **Bake the Pastry:**  
   Line with baking parchment, add baking beans, and bake for 15 mins. Remove parchment and beans, brush with beaten egg white, and bake for an additional 15-20 mins until golden and crisp. Let cool.  
5. **Assemble the Filling:**  
   Spread half of the caramel on the pastry base, layer with sliced bananas, and cover with the remaining caramel. Chill in the fridge.  
6. **Finish and Garnish:**  
   Whip the cream until thick and spoon it over the tart. Garnish with grated chocolate before serving.

## Enjoy!
Serve chilled and enjoy this delightful dessert!
